the problem

The water situation is not good - they're working on it. In the meantime, when there is a boil-water advisory, it can take a while to get informed, risking your safety.


the solution

We monitor a number of sources to get timely information. An SMS alert will automatically be pushed to your phone when an advisory is enacted or lifted. Sign up and sip safely my friends!

Other stuffs...

Note that this service is not the source of truth for boil-water advisory notices and isnot associated with the any government entity or department of health. We will simply monitor multiple offical channels and push an SMS notfication to your phone when an advisory is institutied or lifted.
