the problem

The water situation is not good - they're working on it. In the meantime, when there is a boil-water advisory, it can take a while to get informed, risking your safety.


the solution

We monitor a number of sources to get timely information. An SMS alert will automatically be pushed to your phone when an advisory is enacted or lifted.


Text "opt-in" to (555) 555-5555 to receive SMS alerts when boil-water advisories are enacted or lifted. It's that simple. There's no cost.
